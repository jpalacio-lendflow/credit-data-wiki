Company Information

General Information​

General information about the company.

Control Status​

Operating Status - The high level categorization of the entity's functional state or trading status.

Operating Sub Status - Records the specific status of the subject company.

Detailed Operating Status - Records the further low level status of the subject company, in terms of its operations.

Summary​

The details of the editorial comments for the entity.

Company Name​

Current Names - The details of the names the organization trades under for commercial purposes.

Multilingual Names - The details of the organization's official address as recorded with an external authority to which all official correspondence must be sent

Former Primary Names - The details of the entity's former names.

Former Registered Names - The name by which this entity was previously registered at a local or national registry.

Contact Info​

The details of the contact information used for communication with the company.

Adresses​

Primary Address - The details of the Primary Address at which the organization is located or conducts operations.

Mailing Address - The details of the address used by the entity for mailing purposes.

Registered Address - The details of the organization's official address as recorded with an external authority to which all official correspondence must be sent.

Stock Exchanges​

The details of the Stock Exchanges on which the entity's shares are traded.

Registration Numbers​

The details of the organization's identifiers issued by a recognized external authority to uniquely identify subjects. The issuing authority may be a Registration Authority, Government Department or Commercial Enterprise (e.g., Chamber of Commerce numbers, VAT numbers).

Industry Codes​

The details of the business activities in which the organization is actively engaged using a standardized coding schemes such as European NACE and U.S. Standard Industry Classification (SIC).

UNSPSC Codes​

The details of several products and services represented in the United Nations Standard Products and Services Code (UNSPSC) scheme; this scheme was jointly developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and Dun & Bradstreet Corporation in 1998.

Activities​

The details of the descriptions of operations in different languages.

Operations​

Details of the entity's operation.

Number of Employees​

The details of the number of individuals engaged by a business for various purposes, e.g., to perform its business operations.

Financials​

The details of the standardized summary view of the financial results (actual or estimated) for the organization.

Banks​

The details of banks or other financial institutions at which the entity has an account.

Competitors​

The details on the entities that are identified as the competitors of the subject entity.

Customers​

Details who the customers of this organization are.

Business Trust Index​

Records the likelihood of a business attempting evasion.
