Feedback

The feedback endpoint enables you to send transaction data to Socure.

referenceId​

The Reference ID is an identifier unique to this transaction.

Initial Decision​

The decision was made prior or simultaneously with the decision to approve an applicant applying for a product.

Final Decision​

The decision was made after the applicant was approved for an account

Final Fraud Outcome​

Identifies whether a transaction was determined to be fraudulent based on an application's current status. Socure uses this information to train our models and inform fraud rate variables

Fraud Reported Date​

Date when fraudulent activity was reported

Fraud Type​

  • 1st party - 1 - Products and Services Fraud - Credit Repair, Bust Out
  • 1st party - 2 - Relationship and Trust Fraud - Money Mules, Work from Home Scams, Romance Scams
  • 1st party - 3 - Embezzlement - Employee Fraud
  • 1st party - 4 - False Claim - True Name fraud
  • 1st party - 5 - Synthetic ID - Person Doesn't Exist - Fake Identity
  • Account Take Over - 6 - Account Take over with compromised credentials - Victim's phone, email or other authentication sources compromised
  • 3rd party - 10 - Origination fraud with compromised credentials - Victim's phone, email or other authentication sources compromised
  • 3rd party - 11 - Impersonated Authorized Party (Note: Default 3rd party fraud type)
  • 3rd party - 12 - Physical Alteration - Selfie Spoofing, Selfie Mismatch
  • 3rd party - 13 - Digital Payment (Note: Typically not applicable)
  • 3rd party - 14 - Physical Forgery/Counterfeit - Fake Documents submitted

Loss Amount​

Gross loss amount recorded as a result of fraud, not including recovered funds

Channel​

Channel in which the transaction application was submitted.

Manual Review​

Boolean flag indicating whether an applicant or customer was sent to manual review.

Step Up Auth​

An array containing the types of authentication which have been passed.
