Cashflow Analysis​

From a lending perspective, “cash flow” represents the total net amount of cash available to the business for servicing debts, as well as growing the business assets. The cash flow tells an underwriter how much debt the business can successfully handle and how much cash flow is left to be reinvested into the business. Cash flow provides potential lenders with a picture of the business’s financial capacity to pay back a loan. Cash flow data are highly predictive of credit risk and loan performance. It is one of the most promising options for improving automated underwriting because it provides a more detailed and timely picture of how applicants manage their finances than traditional credit reports.
