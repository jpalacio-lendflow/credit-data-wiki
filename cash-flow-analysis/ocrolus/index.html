Ocrolus

Estimated Revenue​

The estimated revenue of the business in a calendar month. This is calculated by summing together all credit transactions, then removing all transfer credits (except for wire transfers), all alternative lender credits (except for factoring credits), and all transactions that contain words that signify that they are not revenue deposits. This includes words such as Refund, Reversal, Cash Back, Return, and about 20 others.

Deposit Sum​

The total monetary value of credits.

Withdrawal Sum​

The total monetary value of debits.

Avg Daily Balance​

The average daily balance including weekends.

Deposit Count​

The total number of credit transactions.

Avg Deposit​

The average monetary value of deposits in a statement period.

Weekday Negative Balances​

The list of all weekdays in which the daily ending balance fell below zero in a calendar month.

Weekday Avg Daily Balance​

The average daily balance of weekdays.

Negative Balances​

The list of dates with balances below zero (including weekends).

Transaction Count​

The total number of transactions in a statement period.

NSF Count​

The number of unique NSF & Overdraft fees for each bank account. This is especially useful with institutions that lump NSF fees into one transaction.

Number of Alt Lending Debits​

A list of all debit transactions involving small business lenders, personal lenders, mortgage players, and others in the online lending space. This list also includes debit transactions relating to Merchant Cash Advance and Factoring, though these are separately filtered in unique lists for Merchant Cash Advance Transactions and Factoring Transactions.

Value of Alt Lending Debits​

The total monetary value of alternative lending debits. Alternative lenders can include small business lenders, personal lenders, mortgage players, and others in the online lending space.

Number of Alt Lending Credits​

A list of all credit transactions involving small business lenders, personal lenders, mortgage players, and others in the online lending space. This list also includes credit transactions relating to Merchant Cash Advance and Factoring, though these are separately filtered in unique lists for Merchant Cash Advance Transactions and Factoring Transactions.

Value of Alt Lending Credits​

The total monetary value of alternative lending credits. Alternative lenders can include small business lenders, personal lenders, mortgage players, and others in the online lending space.

Transactions​

A list of all transactions to/from alternative lenders, including small business lenders, personal lenders, mortgage players, and others in the online lending space. This list also includes transactions relating to Merchant Cash Advance and Factoring, though these are separately filtered in unique lists for Merchant Cash Advance Transactions and Factoring Transactions.

Withdrawal Court​

The total number of debit transactions in a statement period.
