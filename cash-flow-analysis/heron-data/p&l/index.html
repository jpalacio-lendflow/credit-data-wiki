P&L

Revenue​

  • Sales = Sum of sales during specified time period
  • Refunds/Returns = Sum of refunds/returns issued during specified time period

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)​

  • Beginning Inventory = Sum of inventory at beginning of period
  • Purchases = Sum of inventory purchases made (raw materials, etc.) during period
  • Ending Inventory = Beginning Inventory + Purchases - COGS

Gross Profit​

Revenue - COGS

Operational Costs​

  • Advertising & Marketing = Sum of marketing spend during specific time period
  • Payroll and Consultants = Sum of payments to staff, contractors, consultants, benefits, personnel insurance, etc. Note: This is labor costs NOT directly related to the production of a product
  • Entertainment - 100% Business
  • General Expenses = Sum of expenses where it is not possible to assess the purpose e.g. "PayPal" or "Transferwise". These transactions will still have the associated merchant
  • Light, Power, Heating = Sum of expenses related to utilities
  • Motor Vehicle Expenses = Sum of expenses related to company vehicles (maintenance, gas, etc.)
  • Postage, Freight, Courier = Sum of expenses related to shipping, postage, freight
  • Printing and Stationary = Sum of expenses related to printing and office supplies (paper, pens, binders, etc.)
  • Rent = Sum of payments associated with office lease
  • Repairs and Maintenance = Sum of expenses related to maintenance of equipment, office, building, etc.
  • Subscriptions = Sum of costs associated with subscription services (ex: Amazon Prime)
  • Telephone and Internet = Sum of all costs related to business phone line, internet, TV (if applicable)
  • Travel = Sum of costs related to business travel - hotels, flights, gas, Uber/Taxi, food, etc.
  • Audit and Accountancy Fees = Sum of all expenses related to audit and accounting fees Bank Fees Cleaning
  • Insurance = Sum of insurance costs associated with business
  • Software = Sum of costs associated with software/infrastructure services
  • Credit Card = Sum of expenses spent on credit cards that are NOT associated with company
  • Charges/Fees = Sum of bank and finance charges and fees
  • Other Expenses = Sum of any other miscellaneous expenses that do not fall under any of the operational costs categories
  • Non-Operational Costs = Sum of all expenses that are NOT associated with operating the business

Non-Operational Costs​

  • Interest = Effective Interest Rate * Amount of Debt Outstanding
  • Taxes = Effective Tax Rate * Taxable Income
